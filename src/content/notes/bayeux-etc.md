Last night, <cite>The Simpsons</cite> had fun with the [Bayeux Tapestry](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayeux_tapestry). You can [have fun with it](https://htck.github.io/bayeux/), too.

All was not fun with last night's episode, though: in their parody of presidential politics, all of the candidates --- Republican and Democrat --- were *old white men!* That's not the case for the Democrats this time around, with Barack Obama, Bill Richardson, and Hillary Clinton all running.
