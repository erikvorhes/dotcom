Alan Liu, et al., from UC--Santa Barbara have put together a digital archive of [<cite>Agrippa (a book of the dead)</cite>](http://agrippa.english.ucsb.edu/), the revolutionary collaboration by William Gibson, David Ashbaugh, and Kevin Begos Jr. It's incredible.
