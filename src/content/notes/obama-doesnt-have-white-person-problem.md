I'm completely sick of the conventional wisdom that Barack Obama somehow has a problem with "hard-working white Americans."[^1] [He didn't have this problem in Oregon](http://tpmelectioncentral.talkingpointsmemo.com/2008/05/oregon_exit_polls_obama_handil.php), let alone the other mostly-white states where he's won. Instead, as [Josh Marshall notes](http://www.talkingpointsmemo.com/archives/194870.php), he doesn't do well with white voters from Appalachia --- relatively small potatoes in the general election --- but an "Appalachian problem" doesn't have the same sense of urgency as what the media & the Clinton campaign have construed.

[^1]: I mean, *I'm* white, but I sometimes wonder about the "hard-working" part.
