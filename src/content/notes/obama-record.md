I'm voting for Obama tomorrow in the Illinois primary, because I think he's the best candidate out there --- and I'm looking forward to feeling *good* about my vote. (See [<cite>The Morning News</cite>](http://www.themorningnews.org/archives/the_guilfoilewarner_papers/if_its_tuesday_this_must_be_2008.php) for more about this!)

If Obama doesn't get the Democratic nomination, I'll be faced with a quandary: none of the Republican candidates are even remotely appealing. But I don't know if I could bring myself to vote for Hillary Clinton, whose campaign would have won the nomination in no small part because of [race bating](http://tnr.com/politics/story.html?id=d8d3f623-f2af-436b-9273-5a85315f4e85).

I hope I won't have to face that choice.
